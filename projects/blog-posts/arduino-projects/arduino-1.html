Arduino HW 1 - Get to know your tools

IGME 470

This was my first real project, being a simple button to turn on and off a light. It was very interesting using actual wires and grounding and currents. To be completely honest, it reminded me slightly of Minecraft redstone.

The resistors and the jumper wires reminded me of using colored wool to organize wires and redstone comparators to set the signal strength.
