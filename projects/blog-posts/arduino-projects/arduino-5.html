Arduino HW 5 - Mood Cue

IGME 470

Homework 5 has been my favorite homework so far, as it is simple and I can see straightforward ways of how this knowledge converts to other projects. The Mood Cue assignment uses a potentiometer to change the angle of a servo motor. It is intended to point at a sheet of paper with different zones representing different moods (though I don't have that).

One frustration that my fellow classmates and I ran into is that the male to female connector for the servo motor didn't quite fit with the motor's header, so I had to hold it with one hand while testing. Other than that, this project was pretty straightforward and fun to see the motor move from my own inputs.
